## Overview

Expo is a professional developer platform with a luminous, confidence-radiating design built on a monochromatic palette with cool blue-gray tones. The design system embodies a clean, developer-focused aesthetic with Expo Black as the signature color.

---

## Colors

### Foundation

The palette is monochromatic with cool blue-gray undertones. Expo Black (#000000) for headlines and CTAs, Cloud Gray (#f0f0f3) for the page background, and cool neutrals for structure.

### Action Tones

- **Primary — Expo Black (#000000)**: Primary headlines, CTA buttons.
- **Secondary — Cool Gray (#60646c)**: Secondary text and muted elements.
- **Accent — Link Cobalt (#0d74ce)**: Links and interactive signals.

### Surface Hierarchy

| Level | Light | Dark | Use |
|-------|-------|------|-----|
| Background | #f0f0f3 | #171717 | Page canvas |
| Surface | #ffffff | #1a1a1a | Cards, panels |

## Do's and Don'ts

### Do

- ✅ Use Expo Black (#000000) for primary headlines and CTAs
- ✅ Use Inter consistently — clean developer typography
- ✅ Maintain cool blue-gray undertones throughout
- ✅ Keep layouts clean and uncluttered

### Don't

- ❌ Don't introduce warm colors — maintain cool monochrome
- ❌ Don't use heavy shadows — keep elevation subtle
- ❌ Don't use decorative elements — developer focus
